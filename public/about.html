Our Story

From One Loom to a Legacy

New Shelton Hosiery began in 2005 in a small workshop in Gujranwala — the heart of Pakistan's textile industry. Our founder, Mrs. Sunita Shelton, started with a single loom and a dream: to craft sweaters that every Pakistani woman would cherish.

What began as a passion project grew into a beloved brand. Today, we serve over 50,000 happy customers across Pakistan, with a collection of 200+ unique designs crafted from premium wool, cashmere, and blended yarns.

Every stitch we make carries the warmth of our founders' vision — quality you can feel, style you can wear with confidence.

Sunita Shelton
