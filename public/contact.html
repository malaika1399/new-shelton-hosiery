Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging. Please contact us to initiate a return.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above Rs. 999. For orders below Rs. 999, a flat shipping fee of Rs. 79 applies.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days for most locations. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select cities at an additional cost.
Are the products genuine?
Absolutely. Every product is manufactured by us at our own facility in Gujranwala. We use 100% genuine premium yarns and provide quality assurance on every piece.
Do you offer bulk/wholesale orders?
Yes, we welcome wholesale and bulk orders. Please email us at [email protected] or call us to discuss pricing and minimum order quantities.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also log in to your account to view your order status anytime.
